# 🏏 TurfMate

> **Status: 🚧 Under Development**

TurfMate is a web application for booking cricket turf slots — making it easy for players to find, check availability, and reserve turf time without the hassle of phone calls or walk-ins.

---

## 💡 The Idea

Finding and booking a cricket turf in your city is still a frustratingly manual process — you call the owner, hope the slot is free, and confirm over WhatsApp. TurfMate aims to fix that.

The goal is a simple, clean platform where:
- **Players** can browse nearby turfs, check real-time slot availability, and book instantly.
- **Turf Owners** can manage their listings, set pricing, block dates, and track bookings from a dashboard.

---

## 🎯 Core Features (Planned)

- 🔍 **Browse & Search** — Find turfs by location, size, or availability
- 📅 **Slot Booking** — Pick a date and time slot and book in a few clicks
- 👤 **User Accounts** — Register/login for players and turf owners
- 🏟️ **Turf Listings** — Each turf has its own page with details, photos, and pricing
- 📊 **Owner Dashboard** — Manage bookings, availability, and turf info
- 🔔 **Booking Confirmations** — Notifications for confirmed or cancelled bookings
